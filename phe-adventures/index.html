About

An abandoned game that never left it's prototype phase. The game is a modded version of PurrCatory and was originally intended to be a 2D fighting game similar to a certain famous crossover series... This itself being a crossover of 3 companies. (Which at this point, only one of them was even registered). the PHE refers to Team PHE. A random idea that I once had for a parent company for Pikakid98 Studios, Hand In The Cookie Jar Productions (Now known as Not-So Classic Games) and EyeShellGames. The idea was meant to be a crossover fighting game with characters such as Candice (The Story Of A Succubus Named Candice), Ayden (Cat Warfare) and some kind of character from EyeShellGames. This idea quickly fell through as (shockingly) nobody was interested in putting their trust in me to sell out to a parent company made by me
