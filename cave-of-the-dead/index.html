About

Cave Of The Dead (Originally known as Cave Explorer) was my first ever game and was created for a game design course back at an old school. What was originally supposed to be 2 simple rooms in order to pass turned into a whole (yet still short) little game that surprised even the teacher when I wanted to continue even after passing the test.

Engine: Game Maker 8.1

Plot: Cave Of The Dead takes place in the infamous (in universe) cave of the same name. A cave filled with the tortured souls of the deceased and ruled by an evil demon. Take control of Mike Caver (That was a name I came up with for the cancelled sequel.) as you explore the cave to save your best friend from the evil cave of the dead... Also the controls are terrible so be careful.

The "Classic" build is an updated version. The original build will henceforth be referred to as "Cave Of The Dead OG"
