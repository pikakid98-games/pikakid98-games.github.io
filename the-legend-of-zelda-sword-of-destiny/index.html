About

The second game that I ever made and heavily inspired by both The Legend Of Zelda: Twilight Princess, Skyward Sword and Ocarina Of Time.. Specifically the 3D version that I was playing at the time. The game is intended as a sequel to Ocarina Of Time. At the time of making this, I was unaware that Majora's Mask was even a thing until the 3D version came out. It uses all of the collectable items from Ocarina Of Time with areas (VERY poorly) redesigned from OOT
